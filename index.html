About Me

My name is David Bragg.

I was a military brat born in Seoul, S. Korea. Now I call Federal Heights, Colorado home! We have great personal relationships with the Mayor, City Council members, the Fire Chief, the Chief of Police, and it’s a wonderful place to live.

My wife, Megan, and I are passionate about giving back to our community. Every October we raise food for Food Bank of the Rockies with our charity haunted house in our garage. Over 3,000 lbs. of food raised so far! Through The Alliance we’ve built fences for a no-kill animal rescue, made blankets for Children’s Hospital, packed school lunches for children living below the poverty line, and packed meals for Syrian refugees displaced by war.
